  ## Architecture and Interior Design Photographer in Paris, France
  
  As an English-speaking architecture photographer based in the Paris region, I collaborate with international architects, developers, interior designers, and global agencies to translate the unique character of each project into images. My work goes beyond mere documentation; it is about revealing your architectural intent, the dialogue between materials, and the finesse of high-end interior design and bespoke furnishings.

  **Visual expertise dedicated to your indoor and outdoor spaces**
  Through a rigorous mastery of perspective and natural light, I strive to accurately render volumes, textures, and custom furniture. Whether for new structural developments, complex heritage renovations, or luxury interior design projects across France and major European hubs, each photo shoot is tailored to highlight the designers' vision and elevate the excellence of your work.
