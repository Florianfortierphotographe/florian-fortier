  ## Corporate and industrial photography
  
  As a corporate photographer based in the Paris region, I help companies, industries, and artisans build their visual image libraries. Complementing my architectural work, I produce authentic corporate photography reports that document your complex construction sites, technical expertise, and the dynamism of your teams.

  **Highlighting your events and employer branding**
  Whether for a building inauguration, construction monitoring, or professional lifestyle portraits, I immerse myself in your world to capture its essence. My goal is to provide professional photographs that humanize your corporate communication and strengthen your brand image with international partners and employees.

  I operate in Paris, the Île-de-France region, and throughout France to cover your corporate events, construction sites, or daily operations. Contact me to share your brief and design a tailor-made photography report.
